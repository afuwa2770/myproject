Biography

Dr.Sheikh Anas Kaliisa, the son of Haji Abdunoor Kaliisa and Hajat Hanifa Kaliisa,
was born in 1950 in Southwestern Uganda where he spent his formative years
before being transfered to Sseke, a town near Masaka, Uganda to study religion.
Later in his teanage years, he was transfered to Kawempe Kampala for his high
school studies after which he got a scholarship to studay islamic law at Medina
University in Saudi Arabia in the late 1960s. He later studied secular law and
obtained two advanced degrees, including a PhD from Makere University in
Uganda.

After he graduated from Madina University with a degree in Sharia Law, he
moved back to Uganda in 1972, where Idd Amin Dada had just taken over as
a president. Dr. Sheikh Anas Kaliisa was immediately drafted as the personal
tutor of president's children. He gained the presidet's confidence that he appointed
him to become the youngest Deputy Muft of the Ugandan Muslim Supreme council
at the age of 23years. Dr.Sheikh Anas Kaliisa continued to serve his country till his
demise on 4th November 2020. May the almighty forgive him.

Accomplishements and Roles he Played Over Years:

  • He was the director of religious Affairs at Ugandamuslim supreme council.
  • He was a lecturer at Makerere University.
  • He was a deputy Muft and chief kadhi, at Uganda muslim
    Supreme council.
  • He was a tutor and religious advisor at the office of

  • He was a vice Rector and Lecturer at the islamic University
    of Uganda.
  • He was the secretary of the Eminent council of Sheikhs in Uganda.
  • He was the chairman of Muslim JUrists in Uganda.
  • He was a penelist on Uganda's premier television, NBS on a
    top-rated where he used to dissect key issues around
    the world, and used to run every sunday evening.
  • Host of several programs on Salam Tv, the highest rated islamic
    television in Uganda.
  • Managing Director of the Institute of Research and Training.
  • Founding member and chairman of Salam Charity, responsible for
    constructing numerous schools and mosques in Uganda.
  • Founding Member and Managing Director House of Zakat and Waqf
    in Uganda.
  • Member of the International Islamic Fiqh Academy, an Islamic
    Institution for the advanced study of Islamic jurisprudence
    and law based in Jedda, Saudi Arabia. He represented Africa at the academy.
  • Along with Sheikh Abdunoor, Dr.Sheikh Anas Kaliisa successfully
